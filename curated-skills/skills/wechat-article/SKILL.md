# WeChat Article Writer

Create long-form articles optimized for WeChat Official Account (gongzhonghao) reading experience.

## When to Use

- "Write a WeChat article about [topic]"
- "Help me create gongzhonghao content"
- "Write a long-form post for WeChat"
- Any request for WeChat Official Account content

## When NOT to Use

- Xiaohongshu posts (use `xiaohongshu-writer` — completely different format)
- Short social media updates
- English-language articles
- Academic or technical papers

## Article Structure

### Title (2 options)

Always provide 2 title options:
1. **Hook title**: Curiosity-driven, emotion-triggering
2. **Value title**: Clear benefit or takeaway

Rules:
- Under 30 Chinese characters
- No clickbait that misrepresents content
- Front-load the value proposition

### Opening (first 3 lines)

The opening shows in preview — it determines whether readers tap in:
- Start with a relatable scenario, question, or surprising fact
- NO greetings like "hello everyone" or "today I want to talk about"
- Get to the point within 3 lines

### Body Sections

Structure with clear section headers (use bold or emphasis, not H1-H6 which WeChat doesn't render well):

```
**Section Title**

Content paragraph (2-4 sentences max per paragraph)

Key point highlighted in bold or quotes

Another short paragraph with supporting detail
```

Rules:
- One core idea per section
- 3-5 sections for a standard article (1500-3000 characters)
- Use transitional phrases between sections
- Include at least one concrete example or case per section

### Closing

- Summarize the key takeaway in 1-2 sentences
- End with a question or call to action (encourage comments)
- Optional: tease next article topic

## Formatting for WeChat

- Short paragraphs (2-4 sentences max — mobile reading)
- Line break between every paragraph (WeChat renders without paragraph spacing)
- Bold key phrases for skimming
- Use quotation blocks for emphasis (WeChat renders these as gray boxes)
- No markdown headers (WeChat doesn't support them) — use bold text instead

## Output Format

```
Title Option A: [hook title]
Title Option B: [value title]

---

[Opening: 2-3 lines, hook the reader]

**[Section 1 Title]**

[Content]

**[Section 2 Title]**

[Content]

**[Section 3 Title]**

[Content]

[Closing with CTA]
```

## Tone Guidelines

- Knowledgeable but approachable
- First person singular or editorial "we"
- Confident assertions backed by examples
- Conversational but not overly casual (this isn't xiaohongshu)
- Avoid jargon unless the target audience is professional

## Notes

- Always write in Chinese unless explicitly asked otherwise
- Article length sweet spot: 1500-3000 Chinese characters (3-6 minute read)
- WeChat readers typically read on mobile — optimize for small screens
- If the user provides a topic without specifics, ask about target audience and key message before writing
